HLS

    HLS Data platform is a cloud-based data analytics and management solution designed to support organizations in processing and analyzing large volumes of data from various sources, including satellite imagery, weather data, social media feeds, and more.

  • HLS stands for "Humanitarian, Security, and Environmental applications" and the platform was developed by the European Space Agency (ESA) to support humanitarian, security, and environmental applications.
  • It provides users with access to a range of tools and services for data processing, analysis, visualization, and sharing..
  • HLS Data platform is used by a range of organizations and individuals, including government agencies, NGOs, and researchers, to support a variety of applications, such as disaster management, food security, and environmental monitoring.
  • It also offers various data products that can be used for monitoring and decision-making purposes, such as land cover maps, water quality maps, and vegetation indices.
