Data Engineering

Data engineering is the process of designing, building, and maintaining the systems and infrastructure necessary to support the processing, storage, and analysis of large volumes of data. It involves a combination of skills in computer science, statistics, and mathematics, as well as an understanding of business needs and processes.

Data Analytics

Data analytics is the process of examining and analyzing large data sets to uncover useful information and insights that can be used to inform decision-making and improve business performance. It involves using statistical and computational methods to extract meaningful patterns and trends from data, as well as techniques for cleaning and transforming data to make it more accessible for analysis.

Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that involves the extraction, analysis, interpretation, and visualization of structured and unstructured data. It combines elements of statistics, mathematics, computer science, and domain expertise to extract insights and knowledge from data.

Data Ingesting

Data ingestion refers to the process of collecting, importing, and processing raw data from various sources into a data storage system, such as a database or data warehouse. The data is typically in various formats and structures, and the ingestion process is responsible for converting it into a format that can be easily analyzed and utilized by data analysts, scientists, and other users.?

Migration

Migration refers to the process of moving from one place to another, often for the purpose of settling in a new location for an extended period of time. This movement may be voluntary or involuntary and can be driven by various factors such as economic, social, political, or environmental reasons. People may migrate within a country, or they may cross international borders and become immigrants or emigrants. Migration can have significant impacts on both the individuals who move and the communities they leave behind and settle in.
